Our Facilities

Technology Tower

Technology Tower at Vellore Institute of Technology is a cutting-edge facility that hosts advanced computer labs, IoT and AI research centers, along with specialized laboratories for the ECE department, including VLSI, Embedded Systems, and Communication labs, providing students with hands-on learning and research opportunities.

View Details
Silver Jubilee Tower

Silver Jubilee Tower at Vellore Institute of Technology is a modern academic block equipped with spacious lecture halls, seminar rooms, and advanced research labs, fostering an environment conducive to learning, innovation, and academic excellence.

View Details
Pearl Research Park

The PRP Block at VIT Vellore is one of the newer academic buildings, primarily catering to freshers. Apart from its enormous and spacious classrooms, the block features brilliantly designed smart classrooms and galleries, adding to its modern infrastructure. The interior is well-designed, making it a comfortable and advanced learning space for students.

View Details
Sri M Vishweshwaraiah Building

SMV is also called 'The Hexagon' due to its unique architecture. The building is in the shape of a hexagon consisting of spacious classrooms and well-equipped labs, mostly Chemical and Biotechnology Labs. SMV houses the School of Bio-sciences and Technology (SBST), Catering and Hotel Management.
