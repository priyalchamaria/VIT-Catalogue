PEARL RESEARCH PARK

The Pearl Research Park ( PRP ) is one of the unique building at VIT. Which consists of G+7 floors with total built-up area of 10.53 Lakh sqft. Located between SJT Building and Gandhi Block. It was inaugurated by Hon'ble Thiru M.K. Stalin, Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu. on 01st February, 2023 in the presence of Hon'ble Thiru. Duraimurugan, Minister of Water Resources, Hon'ble Thiru. Dr. K. Ponmudy, Minister of Higher Education and Hon'ble Thiru. R. Gandhi, Minister of Handlooms & Textiles and Dr. G Viswanathan, Hon'ble Chancellor. Apart from enormous and spacious classrooms, brilliantly designed smart classrooms and galleries added to the list of unique features of PRP.

Best Interiors are provided for all laboratories and research centres of various departments with international standards.
